# Aisle Planner UX bake-off

**Status:** TEMPLATE — needs you to spend 2 hours in their product.

The audit flagged "Aisle Planner UX bake-off" as a Tier-D item. This template gives you the structure to fill in once you've actually used Aisle Planner end-to-end with a fake wedding.

## Why this matters

Aisle Planner sells to wedding planners (not venues directly), but planners often recommend Aisle Planner to the venue's couples. If a couple uses Aisle Planner with their planner AND Bloom with their venue, the question is: does the venue's Bloom-driven workflow feel competitive, ahead, or behind Aisle Planner's?

If Bloom feels behind: couples push the venue to "just use Aisle Planner like our planner does." Bloom loses on couple-side stickiness.

If Bloom feels ahead: couples push their planner to "use Bloom like our venue does." Bloom expands into the planner segment.
