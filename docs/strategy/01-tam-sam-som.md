## What kills these numbers

- HoneyBook ships an in-product brain layer that's good enough → SAM compresses by 30-50%
- A wedding-venue-specific CRM emerges and bundles a brain → similar
- Industry contracts (recession, generational decline in marriage rates) → TAM shrinks
- Bloom can't compete with platform-bundled AI on price → SOM compresses

## What expands these numbers

- B&B / corporate / retreat venue verticals (per `12-expansion-verticals.md`) → TAM grows ~3-5x
- The brain-as-a-service shape lets Bloom sell to non-venue users (planners, photographers as "intelligence on top of your bookings") → TAM expands beyond venues
- International expansion (UK, AU, NZ wedding markets) → SAM grows ~2x
